The Children’s Univeristy Foundation

As head of communications, I was leading creative campaigns and projects with design studios and freelancers.

Role: Head of Communications & Design Lead (2018-2022) Team: The Children’s University Team, Collaborations: Openform Studio, Motionful Studio, Hania Kmieć Illustration, Agencja Niedoogarnięcia, TOFU Studio, Harry Slogan,Tools: Asana, Adobe Suite, Google Suite, Mural, Design Thinking, meetings and workshops :)

OVERVIEW & BUSINESS CONTEXT

The Children’s University Foundation is one of the biggest non profit organization from Poland in the educational sector. It reaches over 80 000 teachers in Polish primary schools, and over 30 000 children during dedicated workshops.

MISSION

As the reasearch proves, school is killing curiosity, and curiosity is crutial in the learning process. The Children’s University Foundation in 2007 set a goal to change it. The need is still valid today, yet the circumstances and schools have changed a lot.

MY ROLE & CHALLENGES

The biggest challenge during the four years in the Children’s University Foundation was to create consistent communication and keep up with the constant changes, especially during the pandemic, and switch to online teaching. The second challenge was to build a strong brand with limited resources, which is an often struggle in the NGO world.

I was leading the communication team (design, copywriting, UX, PR, social media, and marketing).
