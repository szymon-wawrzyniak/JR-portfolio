Hello! I’m happy you’re here.

My name is Justyna Rawińska. I’m a designer with experience in marketing, branding, translations, and design thinking in use. I work as a freelance designer and part-time journalist in a local radio station.

I also have worked for the Children’s University Foundation (head of communications), White Raven Academy (brand manager and UX person), and L.I.F.E. Argentina (volunteers coordinator).

I grew up in Poland in the 90s. I was lucky enough to travel the world and live abroad, and because of these experiences, I have a unique perspective on modern societies and the systemic changes we are going through. Nature is my biggest inspiration.

Once I heard that I have a "crazy social perspective on everything", and that actually can be my design superpower I use it also to run interviews for my podcast.

I’d be happy to hear from you!
justyna.rawinska@gmail.com

I love collaborating with startups, creative individuals, NGOs, and socially aware companies. Being a good designer means always working in the best interest of humans and nature.
